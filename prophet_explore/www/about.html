About

Prophet Explorer is a Shiny App that offers an interactive interface to explore the main functions of [prophet Package] ; an open source software released by Facebook's Core Data Science team.

To explore:
  • Upload your data in the right format (dataframe with at least two columns 'ts' & 'y' and an additional column 'cap' in caseoflogistic growth)
  • Tune the parameters
  • Press 'Fit Prophet Model'
